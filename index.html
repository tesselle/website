R packages & archaeology

A collection of R packages for research and teaching in archaeology.

tesselle is a collection of R packages for research and teaching in archaeology. These packages focus on quantitative analysis methods developed for archaeology. The tesselle packages are designed to work seamlessly together and to complement general-purpose and other specialized statistical packages.
