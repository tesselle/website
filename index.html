R packages & archaeology

A collection of R packages for research and teaching in archaeology.

tesselle is a collection of R packages for research and teaching in archaeology. These packages focus on quantitative analysis methods developed for archaeology and can be used to explore and analyze common data types in archaeology: count data, compositional data and chronological data.

The tesselle packages are designed to work seamlessly together and to complement general-purpose and other specialized statistical packages.
